\maketitle
\chapter{Preface}
The preface explains how the handbook should be read and why the front matter
uses roman numerals instead of chapter numbers.

\tableofcontents

\mainmatter
\chapter{Primary Record}
The primary record chapter contains the main numbered narrative about corpus
maintenance, reference generation, and archive review.

\chapter{Working Practice}
The second numbered chapter focuses on routine publication work and the
importance of reproducible reference builds.

\backmatter
\chapter{Afterword}
Backmatter often hosts a concise closing note, index, or bibliography. This
fixture keeps that structure lightweight while still exercising the mode
switch between front matter, main matter, and back matter.
